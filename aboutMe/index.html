The buttons below initiate a few interactive functions I have created. "Start Game" presents you with a series of prompts that ask you for your name, then take you through 10 questions about yourself, each presenting a small opportunity to learn a bit about me. In the end, there is a tally system used to indicate whether or not we would likely be friends (it's a trick, of course we could be friends!). The second button, "Show Answers", is only a simple return of the results from "Start Game". The next game is "Guess My Number", where the system selects a random number (1-100), and you have 4 chances to guess the number! Lastly, we have "Guess Where I've Traveled", where you have 6 chances to guess any one of the countries I have traveled to.

In each of these games, there is no need to use correct capitalization of your letters, and for the yes or no questions, you can use yes/y and no/n.
